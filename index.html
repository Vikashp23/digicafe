Documents Required

Keep these ready to ensure faster processing.

For ITR Filing

  • Salaried: Form 16, salary slips, bank statements, investment proofs, rent details
  • Business/Profession: financial statements, bank statements, GST returns, TDS details
  • Capital Gains: stock statements, sale deeds, capital gain calculations
  • Companies/Firms: balance sheet, P&L, tax audit report (if applicable)

Common Document Checklists

  • PAN: photo & signature, Aadhaar, mobile (Aadhaar-linked), email
  • Driving Licence: photo & signature, Aadhaar, mobile/email, PAN, blood group, 10th proof
  • Digital Signature (DSC): photo & signature, PAN, Aadhaar (XML), mobile & email
  • IRCTC e-ticket: Aadhaar (XML), mobile & email

Document submission can be done via WhatsApp or email based on your preference.
