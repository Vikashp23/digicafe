Documents Required

Quick reference guide for common services

ITR Filing Documents

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Form 16 / Salary Slips
  • Bank Statement
  • Investment Proofs (80C)
  • Previous Year ITR (if any)

GST Registration

  • PAN of Business
  • Aadhaar of Proprietor/Partners
  • Business Address Proof
  • Bank Account Statement
  • Photograph of Owner
  • Board Resolution (if Company)

DSC (Digital Signature)

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Photograph (White Background)
  • Email ID
  • Mobile Number
  • Address Proof
