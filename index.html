Income Tax (ITR) Checklist

  • Basic: PAN, Aadhaar, mobile/email, bank details (IFSC)
  • Verification: Form 26AS + AIS + TIS (TDS/income matching)
  • Salaried: Form 16, salary slips, rent details (if any)
  • Other income: bank interest certificate, FD interest, etc.
  • Deductions: 80C/80D proofs, donations (80G) if applicable
  • Capital gains: stock/MF statement, contract notes; property sale deed
  • Business: P&L/Balance Sheet, bank statements, GST & TDS details (if any)

GST Checklist

  • Registration: PAN, Aadhaar, photo, mobile/email, bank proof
  • Business proof: address proof (bill/lease/NOC), nature of business
  • Returns: sales & purchase invoices, debit/credit notes
  • ITC: GSTR-2B reconciliation + purchase register
  • Notice reply: notice copy + supporting invoices/statements

DSC (Digital Signature) Checklist

  • Photo + Signature
  • PAN
  • Aadhaar Offline XML (or as required)
  • Mobile number + Email address
  • Organisation DSC: authorization/proof (if applicable)

Quick Digital Services

  • PAN: photo/sign, Aadhaar, Aadhaar-linked mobile, email
  • Driving Licence: photo/sign, Aadhaar, PAN, blood group proof, 10th proof
  • IRCTC e-ticket: Aadhaar (XML), mobile & email
