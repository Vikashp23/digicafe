Required Documents (Updated Checklists)

Keep these ready for faster processing and fewer follow-ups.

ITR Filing – Latest Checklist

  • Basic: PAN, Aadhaar, mobile/email, bank account details (IFSC)
  • Income: Form 16 (salary), salary slips, pension statement, interest certificates (FD/SB)
  • Mandatory verification: Form 26AS + AIS + TIS (for TDS, income & reporting match)
  • Bank: bank statements (all accounts) for the FY
  • Deductions: LIC/PPF/ELSS, mediclaim (80D), donations (80G), education loan interest (80E), etc.
  • House property: rent received/paid, home loan interest certificate, municipal tax receipts
  • Capital gains: stock/MF statement, contract notes, property sale/purchase deed & dates
  • Business/Profession: P&L/Balance Sheet, GST returns, TDS details, expense proofs (as needed)
  • Foreign income/assets: details if applicable

Tip: If AIS has wrong entries, feedback can be submitted before final filing.

GST – Registration + Returns

  • Registration: PAN, Aadhaar, photo, mobile/email, bank proof, address proof (electricity bill/lease/NOC)
  • Returns: sales invoices, purchase invoices, credit/debit notes, e-invoice/e-way bill details (if applicable)
  • ITC control: GSTR-2B matching + purchase register reconciliation
  • Payments: challans, RCM details (if applicable)
  • Important: From July 2025, GSTR-3B outward liability is system-locked; corrections via GSTR-1A before filing

Class 3 DSC – Documents

  • Individual DSC: PAN, Aadhaar Offline XML, photo, mobile number, email
  • Organisation DSC (if applicable): authorization/organisation proof + signatory PAN/Aadhaar + address proof
  • Note: Token support available (if required)

MCA / Director Compliance (DIR-3 KYC)

  • DIN details, PAN, Aadhaar, email & mobile (OTP verification)
  • Any required address proof as per MCA filing
  • Update: Effective 31 March 2026, Director KYC compliance moved to once in 3 years (instead of annual)
