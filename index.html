ITR Filing — Checklist

  • Basic: PAN, Aadhaar, mobile/email, bank account details (IFSC)
  • Income: Form 16 (salary), salary slips, pension statement, interest certificates
  • Verification: Form 26AS + AIS + TIS (TDS/income matching)
  • Bank: bank statements (all accounts) for the FY
  • Deductions: LIC/PPF/ELSS, mediclaim (80D), donations (80G), etc.
  • House property: rent details, home loan interest certificate, municipal tax receipts
  • Capital gains: stock/MF statement, contract notes, property deed & dates
  • Business/profession: P&L/Balance Sheet, GST returns, TDS details (as applicable)

GST — Registration + Returns

  • Registration: PAN, Aadhaar, photo, mobile/email, bank proof, address proof (bill/lease/NOC)
  • Returns: sales invoices, purchase invoices, credit/debit notes
  • ITC: GSTR-2B matching + purchase register reconciliation
  • Payments: challans, RCM details (if applicable)
