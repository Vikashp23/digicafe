Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How much time does ITR filing take? ▼
ITR filing typically takes 7 days from document submission. This includes document verification (1 day), ITR preparation (2-3 days), and e-filing (1 day). Express turnaround of 3 days is available for urgent filings with additional charges.
Can I claim LIC and SIP investments for tax deductions? ▼
Yes! Both LIC premiums and SIP investments (ELSS funds) are eligible under Section 80C with a combined limit of ₹1,50,000 per financial year. We help optimize these deductions to maximize your refund. Other eligible investments include PPF, NSC, and Sukanya Samriddhi.
What documents are required for company compliance? ▼
For company compliance, you need PAN, CIN, MOA & AOA, financial statements, bank statements, GST returns, TDS returns, shareholding details, and fixed asset register. We provide a detailed checklist after consultation to ensure nothing is missed.
What happens if LLP Form 11 is not filed? ▼
Failing to file Form 11 by the deadline (30 May) can result in penalties up to ₹10,000 and potential strike-off of the LLP. It's critical to file on time. We ensure timely filing and send reminders well in advance.
How can I track my refund status? ▼
Once ITR is e-filed, you can track refund status on the income-tax website using your acknowledgement number. We monitor your refund and send updates via WhatsApp. Average refund processing time is 30-45 days from e-filing date.
Do you provide consultation before filing? ▼
Absolutely! We offer free pre-filing consultation via WhatsApp or call to understand your tax situation, identify deductions, and plan for tax savings. This helps us prepare accurate and optimized returns.
What is your pricing structure? ▼
We offer transparent, affordable pricing starting from ₹299 for basic ITR filing. Pricing varies based on complexity, income sources, and services needed. We provide quotes before starting work with no hidden charges.
